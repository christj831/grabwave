What is SoundGrab?

SoundGrab is a free, browser-based music downloader that lets you save tracks, albums, and playlists from Spotify and YouTube Music as high-quality 320 kbps MP3 files — complete with embedded metadata like artist, album name, track number, genre, and cover art. No app installs, no sign-ups — just paste a link and go.

⚡

Features

  • 🎵 320 kbps MP3 — Guaranteed constant bitrate for every download
  • 📀 Full Metadata — Artist, album, year, genre, track number & cover art embedded automatically
  • 📁 Batch Downloads — Entire playlists & albums downloaded in one click, delivered as a ZIP
  • 📊 Live Progress — Real-time progress bars for overall job and individual tracks
  • 🔗 Multi-Source — Supports Spotify tracks, albums, playlists + YouTube Music links
  • ❌ Cancel Anytime — Abort running downloads instantly with the cancel button
  • 📱 Mobile Friendly — Fully responsive design, works on any device
📥

How to Download

  1. 1
    Copy the link — Open Spotify or YouTube Music and copy the share URL of a track, album, or playlist.
  2. 2
    Paste it here — Drop the URL into the input field above and hit ⬇ Download.
  3. 3
    Wait for it — Watch the real-time progress bar as SoundGrab fetches, converts, and tags every track.
  4. 4
    Save to device — Your file (or ZIP for playlists) will download automatically when finished.
⚠️

Limits

  • Playlist size Large playlists (100+ tracks) may take a while — SoundGrab downloads sequentially for stability
  • Private playlists Only public Spotify playlists are supported; private ones require API scope that may be restricted
  • Audio source Audio is matched & sourced from YouTube — occasional mismatches may occur for very obscure tracks
  • One job at a time Run one download per session for best performance — wait for current job to finish before starting another
  • Personal use Intended for personal, offline listening only — respect copyright & the artists you love

320 kbps MP3 Quality

Every single song downloaded through SoundGrab is encoded at a constant 320 kbps bitrate — the highest quality MP3 standard. Unlike variable bitrate encoders that fluctuate between 128–256 kbps, SoundGrab forces a true CBR 320 kbps encode on every file, so you get studio-grade clarity for every track in your library. Combined with auto-embedded ID3 tags and album artwork, your downloads are indistinguishable from premium music store purchases.
